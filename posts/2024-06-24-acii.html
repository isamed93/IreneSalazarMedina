Cambridge Consultants at ACII2024!

Organising the workshop From Lab to Life: Realising the Potential of Affective Computing.

June 24th, 2024 Irene
Salazar Medina

I'm very excited to announce that my team will be taking part in this year's Affective Computing and Intelligent Interaction (ACII) conference! This forum is dedicated to research on affective and multimodal human-machine interaction and systems.

This year's theme is Human and Beyond, with the context of humans being in interaction with animals, virtual agents, robots, etc. We will be running a workshop called From Lab to Life: Realising the Potential of Affective Computing. We wish to take the privilege of this time and space to talk about how to take the unique insights and innovations developed in labs and turn that into reliable technology solutions for human users.

In other words, we want to bring academia and industry together in a forum to take part in conversation structured around three theme: (1) ethics and regulations, (2) industry perspectives and (3) academic perspectives.

Want to take part? We are looking for people to send in their two cents (up to two pages) regarding the topic at hand! If you would prefer to simply sit back and listen, keep an eye out for our publications and come to Glasgow to hear it all first-hand.

See you September 2024!
